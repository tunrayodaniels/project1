CSS stands for Cascading Style Sheets. It is used for styling webpages.

Differences between padding, margin and border:

Padding is the space between the content and border.

Margin is the spacing between two border lines.

Border is the line between the padding and the margin.It is used to specify the style, width, and color of an element's border.
